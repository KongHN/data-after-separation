刺激因素：刺激是白色背景上的黑色点阵列。点的数量在 15 到 65 之间。
信心度量表：离散量表，范围从 0 到 10。0 表示 “随机猜测”，10 表示 “非常有信心”。
操作变量：每次试验的刺激呈现时长为 100 毫秒或 2000 毫秒，在每个组块内随机混合；两种时长条件在每个组块的试验中各出现一半。
组块规模：每个组块有 40 次试验，总共 6 个组块。
反馈：无反馈。
缺失值字段：参与者有 10 秒时间使用键盘上的数字小键盘输入他们的估计值。NaN 表示参与者未能及时做出反应。
受试者群体：普通人群。
反应设备：键盘和鼠标。
实验环境：在线进行。
训练：在实验前有 10 次试验。（这些数据未包含在数据文件中）
代码链接：https://github.com/yyyxiang/confidence_central_tendency
特别说明：对于每个参与者，每个组块内的平均刺激量是从 30 到 50 的整数离散均匀分布中随机抽取的（无放回抽样）。对于每个组块，组块内的分布是以平均刺激量为中心的均匀分布，范围在 ±15 之间（有放回抽样）。在进行任何估计之前，每个组块开始时会告知参与者该组块内的平均刺激值。鼓励参与者充分使用信心度量表的范围。如果参与者在随机选择的一次试验中的估计值与刺激量的差值在 2 以内，他们有机会获得基于表现的奖金。

Stimulus: The stimuli were arrays of black dots on a white background. The number of dots ranged between 15 and 65.

Confidence scale: Discrete scale from 0 to 10. 0 indicates "Guessed randomly", 10 indicates "Very confident".

Manipulations: The stimulus duration on each trial was either 100 ms or 2000 ms, intermixed randomly within each block; each of the two duration conditions appeared on half of the trials on each block.

Block size: 40 trials on each block, 6 blocks in total.

Feedback: No feedback.

NaN fields: Participants had 10 s to type in their estimate using the number pad on the keyboard. NaN indicates that a participant failed to respond in time.

Subject population: General population.

Response device: Keyboard and mouse.

Experiment setting: online.

Training: 10 trials prior to the experiment. Not included in the data file.

Link to code: https://github.com/yyyxiang/confidence_central_tendency

Special instructions: For each participant, the average stimulus magnitude within each block was randomly drawn from a discrete uniform distribution on the integers from 30 to 50 (sampled without replacement). For each block, the within-block distribution was a uniform distribution centered at the average stimulus magnitude, ranging between ±15 (sampled with replacement). Participants were informed about the average stimulus value within a block at the beginning of each block, before making any estimates. Participants were encouraged to use the full range of the confidence scale. Participants had the opportunity to earn a performance-based bonus payment if their estimate on a randomly chosen trial was within 2 of the stimulus magnitude.
